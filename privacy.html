Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Ladle & Spoon ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our web app or receive communications from us.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used only to process your orders and keep you informed about Ladle & Spoon.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or sign up through our web app, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number (for delivery coordination and SMS notifications)
  • Your delivery address
  • Your order history and preferences
  • Payment method preference (Venmo or cash — we do not collect or store payment card details)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate our delivery business:

  • Process and confirm your orders
  • Coordinate delivery to your address
  • Send weekly menu announcements and order reminders via email and SMS
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Request feedback on your orders
  • Send re-engagement offers if you haven't ordered recently

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number when placing an order, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Ladle & Spoon including:

  • Weekly menu announcements
  • Order confirmations
  • Delivery reminders
  • Occasional promotional offers

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use trusted service providers (such as Google and Twilio) solely to operate our ordering and communication systems. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain your order history and contact information as long as you remain an active customer. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at LadleandSpoon1024@gmail.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by replying to any email with "unsubscribe"
